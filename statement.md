###### **CHAD Shoes Database Management System**



###### **Problem Statement**

Managing shoe production without a proper system is difficult. Keeping track of raw materials, employees, production, rejected items, and dispatch manually can lead to mistakes, delays, and inefficiency.



###### **Scope of the Project**

The CHAD Shoes program helps manage all aspects of shoe manufacturing. It tracks raw materials, employee records, produced and rejected items, and dispatch operations. It also provides organized data to support better decision-making.



###### **Target Users**

* **Factory managers and supervisors**
* **Inventory and production staff**
* **HR personnel**
* **Logistics and dispatch teams**



###### High-Level Features

* Raw Material Management: Keep track of materials and suppliers to avoid delays.
* Employee Management: Maintain records of staff and salaries.
* Production Tracking: Monitor finished products and inventory levels.
* Rejection Management: Record rejected items and reasons to improve quality.
* Dispatch Management: Ensure products reach the right locations on time.
* Reporting: Access organized data to make informed decisions.
